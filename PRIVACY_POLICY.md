# Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: April 19, 2026_

Thank you for using Pingy, built by **yuroyami**.

## No Data Collection

We value your privacy. This app does **not collect**, **store**, or **transmit** any personal information or usage data. No analytics, tracking, or logging mechanisms are used.

- No account or login is required.
- No personal data is collected or processed.
- No third-party SDKs or services are integrated.
- The only network traffic generated is the ICMP/UDP ping packets you explicitly direct at the hosts you choose. No telemetry is sent anywhere else.

## Permissions

If the app requests any permissions (e.g. internet, network state, storage), they are used **solely for local functionality** (namely, sending ping packets and reading/writing local preferences) and never result in data being sent outside your device to us or any third party.

## Changes to This Policy

If the policy ever changes (which we don't anticipate), we will update this file accordingly and indicate the revision date at the top.
